23 July 1936\n\nUsual acknowledgment received 14th July 1936) 3) Madresfield Show. A letter was read from the Madresfield Field Agricultural Society asking the Board to prohibit Parking on the land on the sides of the road from Barnards Green to Suanford and at the Hall Green corner on Show day. Recommended that the Society be given permission on their own responsibility to post notices prohibiting parking, such notices to be posted in consultation with the Show Secretary. R
